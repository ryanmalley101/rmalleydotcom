Scope

The motivation behind building World Behind the Veil

Walking Gods is meant to have an epic scope and diverse ecology, ripe for many different kinds of high fantasy.

The goal of the project

I am hoping to give my players an epic experience like Skyrim, Dark Souls, and God of War through the lens of D&D.

World Behind the Veil's Unique Selling point

The gods of this world are real, physical presences on the Material Plane. They can be touched, spoken with, and even destroyed.

Theme

Genre

Walking Gods should feel consistently high magic, low-tech. Constant war and conflict stop general technological progress outside the realm of the military.

Reader Experience

Walking Gods feels epic and awe-inspiring, with adventure around each corner. The New, Old, and Present world should feel like distinct and wonderful locales that ocassionally overlap.

Reader Tone

World is Darkish - think Witcher rather than Dark Souls.

Recurring Themes

1. The presence of the gods warping the ecology and cultures of their domains. 2. Calamity strikes quickly, and without warning. 3. Dark and threatening ancient ruins in the forest or jungle

Character Agency

Players should be able to feel completely free to move in any direction they like, confident that they'll be able find adventure wherever they look. Character actions reveraberate for long stretches, but even great feats fade with time.
